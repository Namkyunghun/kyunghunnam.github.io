Proceedings of the 43rd International Conference on Machine Learning, Seoul, South Korea · PMLR 306

FOAM: Frequency and Operator Error-Based Adaptive Damping Method for Reducing Staleness-Oriented Error for Shampoo

Kyunghun Nam, Sumyeong Ahn

FOAM analyzes Shampoo through inverse-preconditioner error and introduces a proxy-driven controller that adapts both damping and eigendecomposition frequency. It is part of a broader research direction on preserving preconditioner quality while reducing numerical cost.

Dimension-Independent Convergence Rate for AdaGrad with Heavy-Ball Momentum

Kyunghun Nam, Sejun Park

This paper studies AdaGrad with heavy-ball momentum as an adaptive scaling method, clarifying how diagonal preconditioning and momentum interact in high-dimensional optimization.
