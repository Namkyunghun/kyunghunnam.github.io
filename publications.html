Dimension-Independent Convergence Rate for AdaGrad with Heavy-Ball Momentum

Kyunghun Nam, Sejun Park

Journal MDPI Mathematics, 2025 (Volume 13, Issue 4)

We establish the first dimension-independent convergence rate for AdaGrad when combined with heavy-ball momentum, demonstrating improved optimization efficiency for high-dimensional problems.

Almost Sure Last Iterate Convergence of Sharpness-Aware Minimization

Kyunghun Nam, Jinseok Chung, Namhoon Lee

Workshop ICLR 2023 Workshop on Mathematical and Empirical Understanding of Foundation Models

We provide theoretical analysis proving the almost sure convergence of the Sharpness-Aware Minimization (SAM) algorithm's last iterate, addressing a key gap in understanding this popular optimization method.
